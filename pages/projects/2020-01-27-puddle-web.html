Background

Puddle Web is a response to My Website is a Shifting House Next to a River of Knowledge. What Could Yours Be?. It is a proof of concept in time-aware and ephemeral sites. Much like SnapChat, notes on Puddle Web will eventually evaporate. I've also experimented with open hours for the site. Sites that open and close are more responsible and fit better into the daily rhythms of our lives.
