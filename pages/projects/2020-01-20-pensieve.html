Background

Taking inspiration from the magical device of the same name in Harry Potter, Pensieve is a prototype of what a website could look like if it automatically recorded an archive of its own construction. You can dive into visual reconstructions of previous commits, removing the labor of documentation from the developer, and making documentation more web-native. It is a response to Vernacular Web and web histories.
