# Background {.page-section}
This is an experiment with the GSAP, an animation library for JS, and Scroll Magic. It is a study in the tweening properties of the library, as well as a comparative study in different forms motion through data-sampling.

# Change of Plans {.page-section}
I was originally going to do an pieces of interactive sports journalism about the 12th stage of the 2016 Tour de France. The Stage is dramatic for several reasons, but due to a high-profile crash at the end of the stage the overall standings were rearranged, forever solidifying a fork in the races timeline.
